About Us

Founded in August 2023, Ishmeet’s Treats was born out of a passion for baking and a dream to share homemade happiness with the community.

At Ishmeet’s Treats, we specialize in crafting cookies, brownies, pastries, cakes, and chocolates. Each item is carefully prepared using high-quality ingredients, ensuring freshness, flavor, and a perfect balance of taste and texture. From everyday indulgences to special occasions, our baked goods are designed to delight both the eye and the palate.

As a professional home bakery, our focus is on bringing creativity and precision to every order. Whether it’s a custom-designed cake for a milestone celebration, a box of decadent brownies for gifting, or a simple cookie to brighten your day, we believe that every treat should be as unique as the person enjoying it.

Our journey is fueled by the love of our customers and the joy of creating something memorable. With every recipe perfected and every detail thoughtfully crafted, Ishmeet’s Treats aims to be your go-to bakery in Amritsar — a place where homemade warmth meets professional standards.
